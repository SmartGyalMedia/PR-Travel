From lush rainforests to historic fortresses

Sights & Landmarks

Puerto Rico is home to a vast array of natural and historical treasures. Explore some of the most iconic sights on the island.

El Yunque Rainforest

The only tropical rainforest in the U.S. National Forest System, known for its biodiversity and hiking trails.

Old San Juan

Walk the 500-year-old blue cobblestone streets and admire the colorful Spanish colonial architecture.

Castillo San Felipe del Morro

A 16th-century citadel built to protect the city from sea attacks. A must-see historical landmark.

Flamenco Beach

Located on the island of Culebra, this beach is consistently ranked as one of the best in the world.
